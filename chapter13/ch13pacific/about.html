About Us

Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort was founded in 2006 with the purpose to provide a unique outdoor experience. From a small family-run hotel, we have grown to become a top notch destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

With support from our dedicated team and loyal guests, we have been able to expand our offerings and create unforgettable experiences for all.

Our Goals

Our goal is to continue to provide exceptional service to our guests. We aim to be a leader in sustainable tourism and to make a positive impact on the local community.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing not just an unforgettable experience, but also everlasting memories for all our guests. While also preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings for future generations.
